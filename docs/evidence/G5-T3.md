## What

`GroupPoller` now caches messages that decode but no current keypair can
decrypt, and retries them when keypairs change (plan §2.3 "cache the envelope,
do not drop; retry when any new keypair arrives"):

- Failures are persisted under `closed_group:{groupPubKey}:undecryptable`
  (bounded at 200 entries); own/undecodable messages are never cached.
- Each poll, after processing fresh messages, the poller re-attempts the cached
  envelopes against the **current** keypairs; those that now decrypt are
  delivered and removed from the cache. This covers keypair rotation and a
  device that polls a message before the matching keypair syncs.
- `#setUndecryptable` deletes the key when the cache drains.
