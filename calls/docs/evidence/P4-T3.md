## Early-media-connect deferral (supervisor fix found by the offline-echo demo)

werift can complete ICE BEFORE the signaling FSM is connectable — observed
deterministically in-process: caller-side media emits "connected" while the
FSM is still `local-pre-offer` (ANSWER in flight), and werift dedupes
emissions so the event is never repeated. Without handling, the call hangs in
`connecting` until the 60 s timeout despite a live media path. The supervisor
now remembers the event (`CallContext.earlyMediaConnected`) and applies
`ice-connected` once accept()/setRemoteAnswer/answer-restart brings the FSM to
`connecting`. Verified via `bun examples/offline-echo.ts` (was: timeout after
60 s; now: connected in ~2.7 s, 100/99 frames round-tripped).
