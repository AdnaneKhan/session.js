## Notes

- The consumer installs with `bun install`: the fork's
  `0.0.57-calls.1` prerelease does not satisfy the calls package's
  `peerDependencies: @session.js/client >=0.0.57` under strict semver
  (prerelease ordering) — bun warns and proceeds; the `file:` tarball
  supplies the package. npm would require `--legacy-peer-deps`. When the
  fork is published as a final `0.0.58` (or the peer range widened to
  include the prerelease), the warning disappears; documented here for
  the publisher (Q7).
- S9 (consumer smoke installs cleanly + the demo works) is satisfied:
  packed packages install in a fresh scratch project and the CallManager
  constructs/disposes over the structural contract.
